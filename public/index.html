Created by Julia Cotter, Colin Hennessey, and Ethan Arrowood

Boston is positioned for great loss in the face of rising sea level. The datasets used plot areas of potential coastal and riverine flooding in Boston under various sea level rise scenarios (9-inch in 2030s, 21-inch in 2050s, and 36-inch in 2070s) at high tide.
